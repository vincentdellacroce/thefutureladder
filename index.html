How to Use This Site
Your Blueprint, Step by Step
9th Grade
Explore. Pick the broad field you're curious about. Start with the intro APs (APHG, AP Euro, AP Bio). Join clubs related to your interest.
10th Grade
Narrow down. If engineering looks right, start building. First robotics project, first math competition. Take AP Calc AB and AP Physics if ready.
11th Grade
Execute. This is the year colleges care about most. Take your hardest APs. Lead your club. Land an internship or research position. Take the SAT/ACT.
12th Grade
Apply with a story. Every element of your application should point toward one coherent picture of who you are and what you'll do in college.
The Rule: Colleges don't admit statistics — they admit narratives. Everything on this site is designed to help you build a coherent, compelling narrative around your chosen field. Pick your major, then work backward.
